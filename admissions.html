Deciding on business schools can be daunting

We're here to help

If you're considering Kellog, please reach out to us. We can introduce you to other veterans, give balanced perspectives toward Kellogg, help prepare your application, attend classes, and arrange for you to meet vets.

If you would like to know more about the KVA, please reach out to us below!

Contact Us!

Areas that we can help you:

Resume Reviews

This is one of the areas that we struggle as veterans seeing as we have never had to build a resume before. Our team will help you craft your resume in the Kellogg style and then provide you with feedback on the content of your resume to ensure that you are conveying the important achievements from your career.

Career Advice

Let's face it.... you have not had to think much about what your next job would be while you were in the service. Now is the time to explore your career options so that you can develop a cohesive story on your application. This will also prepare you for recruiting when you arrive at Kellogg for your first quarter.

Kellogg Culture

We want to make sure you are going to the right MBA program for you. We are happy to talk about Kellogg culture, lifestyle, academics, or whatever else you want to talk about to make sure that you are making the informed choice.
